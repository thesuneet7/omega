The Contrarian

Omega risks becoming a guilt-product if value arrives late while capture/privacy risk arrives immediately. It must win one sharp moment: a credible artifact in 10 minutes. Prioritize citation lock, confidence surfacing, and wedge focus; delay overlays until trust is proven.

The First Principles Thinker

Users buy faster shipping and lower cognitive load, not capture. The key equation is whether trustworthy output beats manual reconstruction daily. Build deliverable-native modes and evidence rails, and measure deliverables shipped rather than hours captured.

The Expansionist

The upside is large if Omega owns workflow-critical outputs with proof and becomes the default pre-send/pre-meeting step. Add evidence graph, missing-proof prompts, and later team templates; remove broad positioning.

The Outsider

Messaging is too technical and weak on user pain. Focus on one killer source-grounded workflow, safer default capture boundaries, and visible outcome metrics users care about.

The Executor

Execution should follow strict sequence: output modes, source-locked trust, reopen-context loops, integration parity, then audience expansion. Broad messaging and generic summaries should be deprioritized.
